Our Story

Anjarai Petti has been crafting authentic South Indian spice blends since 1985, using recipes passed down through generations. Our name comes from the traditional spice box ("Petti") used in South Indian kitchens.

We source the finest ingredients and follow traditional methods to create spice mixes that preserve the authentic flavors of South Indian cuisine.

Every product is carefully prepared with attention to detail, ensuring you get the same quality and taste that our family has enjoyed for decades.
